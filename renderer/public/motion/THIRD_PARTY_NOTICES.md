# Motion Assets

The SVG motion assets in this directory were downloaded from `n3r4zzurr0/svg-spinners`.

Source: https://github.com/n3r4zzurr0/svg-spinners
License: MIT
Copyright: MIT (c) Utkarsh Verma

Downloaded files:

- `repo_pulse.svg` from `svg-css/90-ring-with-bg.svg`
- `data_flow.svg` from `svg-css/bouncing-ball.svg`
- `code_scan.svg` from `svg-css/bars-scale.svg`
- `evidence_pulse.svg` from `svg-css/blocks-scale.svg`
- `spark_burst.svg` from `svg-css/pulse-3.svg`

These files are used as local render assets so video rendering does not depend on a live network request.
